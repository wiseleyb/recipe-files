<ul><li> Noodles 
</li><li> Bean sprouts 
</li><li> Finely sliced green and red peppers 
</li><li> Baby spinach 
</li><li> Finely sliced and seeded red or green chiles 
</li><li> Rocket (arugula) 
</li><li> Sliced scallions 
</li><li> Peeled, seeded and sliced cucumbers 
</li><li> Finely sliced Chinese and Savoy cabbage 
</li><li> Whole sugar snap peas 
</li><li> Mint, basil and coriander (cilantro) 
</li><li> Lightly toasted cashew nuts or sesame seeds, for garnish
</li><li> Thai Dressing: 
</li><li> 4 tablespoons fresh lime juice 
</li><li> 3 tablespoons olive oil 
</li><li> 1 tablespoon sesame seed oil 
</li><li> 1 tablespoon soy sauce 
</li><li> Large pinch brown sugar 
</li><li> 1 tablespoon ginger, peeled and finely chopped 
</li><li> 1/2 clove garlic, finely sliced 
</li><li> 1 red chili, seeded and finely sliced 
</li><li> 1 large handful fresh coriander (cilantro) and basil, chopped
</li></ul>
<p><br />
</p>
<div class="editsection" style="float:right;margin-left:5px;">[<a href="/recipe/index.php?title=Crunchy_Thai_Salad&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Crunchy Thai Salad">edit</a>]</div><a name="Directions"></a><h2>Directions</h2>
<p>Noodles <br />
<br />
Cook the noodles as normal and prepare all the vegetables while the noodles are cooking. When they are done rinse under cold water and add to the salad.<br />
<br />
Thai Dressing: <br />
<br />
Mix all of the ingredients together in a jam jar, shake and pour over the salad. Garnish with cashew nuts or sesame seeds.
