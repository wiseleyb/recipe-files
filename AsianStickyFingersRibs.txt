<div class="editsection" style="float:right;margin-left:5px;">[<a href="/recipe/index.php?title=Asian_Sticky-Fingers_Ribs&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Asian Sticky-Fingers Ribs">edit</a>]</div><a name="Credits"></a><h2>Credits</h2>
<p>by Tom Douglas - Tom's Big Dinners
</p>
<div class="editsection" style="float:right;margin-left:5px;">[<a href="/recipe/index.php?title=Asian_Sticky-Fingers_Ribs&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Asian Sticky-Fingers Ribs">edit</a>]</div><a name="Ingredients"></a><h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul><li> Rub:
</li><li> 4 star anise, ground
</li><li> 1 tbs + 1 tsp kosher salt
</li><li> 2 tsp black pepper
</li><li> 3/4 sake
</li><li> Glaze:
</li><li> 1 tbs peanut or vegetable oil
</li><li> 2 tbs minced garlic
</li><li> 2 tsp peeled and grated ginger
</li><li> 2/3 cup honey
</li><li> 1/2 cup rice wine vinegar
</li><li> 1/4 cup soy sauce
</li><li> 1 tbs chinese chile garlic sauce
</li><li> Garnish: 
</li><li> toasted sesame seeds
</li><li> juliened scallions
</li></ul>
<p><br />
</p>
<div class="editsection" style="float:right;margin-left:5px;">[<a href="/recipe/index.php?title=Asian_Sticky-Fingers_Ribs&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Asian Sticky-Fingers Ribs">edit</a>]</div><a name="Directions"></a><h2>Directions</h2>
<p>Remove membrane from under side of ribs<br />
<br />
Rub ribs a day a head.<br />
<br />
Heat oven to 325.  Place ribs, meat side up, on pan.  Poor sake in pan.  Cover with foil.  Roast for 1.5 hours.  <br />
<br />
For the glaze:  saute garlic and ginger... add remaining ingredients, boild until syrupy.<br />
<br />
Glaze ribs and put under broiler.
</p>
