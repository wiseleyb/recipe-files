<div class="editsection" style="float:right;margin-left:5px;">[<a href="/recipe/index.php?title=Marinades&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Marinades">edit</a>]</div><a name="Credits"></a><h2>Credits</h2>
<p><a href="http://seattletimes.nwsource.com/html/northwestlife/134411453_flavors27.html" title="http://seattletimes.nwsource.com/html/northwestlife/134411453 flavors27.html" class="external text" rel="nofollow">Original Recipe</a>

</p>
<div class="editsection" style="float:right;margin-left:5px;">[<a href="/recipe/index.php?title=Marinades&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Marinades">edit</a>]</div><a name="Directions"></a><h2>Directions</h2>
<p>BEEF <br />
<br />
Hamburgers (1-1/2 pound ground meat) <br />
To a basic recipe add: <br />
<br />
</p>
<pre>1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese and 2 tablespoons finely chopped basil or 2 teaspoons dried basil <br />
</pre>
<p><br />
</p>
<pre>¾ cup grated Swiss cheese and 3 finely chopped sun-dried tomatoes <br />
</pre>
<p><br />
</p>
<pre>¾ cup grated cheddar cheese and ¼ cup chopped pimento <br />
</pre>
<p><br />
</p>
<pre>½ cup crumbled blue cheese and 4 slices cooked and crumbled bacon <br />
</pre>
<p><br />
</p>
<pre>½ cup pizza sauce and 1 teaspoon each dried basil and oregano <br />
</pre>
<p><br />
</p>
<pre>2 tablespoons prepared mustard, 1 tablespoon soy sauce and 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce <br />
</pre>
<p><br />
</p>
<pre>1/3 cup taco sauce, 1 teaspoon dried oregano and 2 teaspoons chili powder <br />
</pre>
<p><br />
Meatloaf (2 pounds ground meat) <br />
<br />
To a basic recipe add: <br />
<br />
</p>
<pre>1 envelope onion-soup mix and ½ cup prepared spaghetti sauce <br />
</pre>
<p><br />
</p>
<pre>½ cup salsa or barbecue sauce or ketchup, 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce, ¾ cup diced pepper jack or cheddar cheese and 1/3 cup sliced black olives <br />
</pre>
<p><br />
</p>
<pre>½ cup chili sauce, 4-ounce can drained, chopped green chilies, 1 teaspoon ground cumin and ¼ cup chopped cilantro <br />
</pre>
<p><br />
</p>
<pre>¼ cup bottled steak sauce, 1 cup crumbled Gorgonzola cheese, 1 teaspoon dried basil and ¼ teaspoon dried thyme <br />
</pre>
<p><br />
</p>
<pre>1/3 cup chopped roasted red bell peppers, 4 sliced green onions, 1 teaspoon each dried oregano and chili powder and ½ cup salsa <br />
</pre>
<p><br />
</p>
<pre>Flank steak (1½ pounds) <br />
</pre>
<p><br />
Marinades: <br />
<br />
¾ cup tamari or soy sauce, 1 tablespoon each prepared horseradish and Dijon mustard <br />
<br />
</p>
<pre>¾ cup bottled teriyaki sauce, 2 teaspoons honey, 1 teaspoon sesame oil, 1 tablespoon minced fresh gingerroot, 1 teaspoon five-spice powder and 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper <br />
</pre>
<p><br />
</p>
<pre>¼ cup balsamic or red wine vinegar, 1 tablespoon each olive oil, ketchup, steak sauce and Worcestershire sauce <br />
</pre>
<p><br />
</p>
<pre>1 cup sweet-and-sour barbecue sauce, 3 tablespoons lime juice, 2 tablespoons water, 1 tablespoon minced fresh gingerroot <br />
</pre>
<p><br />
</p>
<pre>1½ cups smoky barbecue sauce, 1 crushed clove garlic, ¼ cup sliced green onions, ¼ cup each water and red wine <br />
</pre>
<p><br />
</p>
<ul><li> After the meat has marinaded discard the marinade. <br />
</li></ul>
<p><br />
<br />
Roast beef (3 to 5 pounds) <br />
<br />
Rubs and coatings - mix and then rub on meat: <br />
<br />
</p>
<pre>¼ cup each Dijon and grainy mustards, 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar, 1 teaspoon coarsely ground black pepper, 1 tablespoon each olive oil and soy sauce <br />
</pre>
<p><br />
</p>
<pre>2 tablespoons brown sugar, 2 teaspoons ground black pepper, ½ teaspoon each salt, thyme, ground ginger, ground allspice and nutmeg <br />
</pre>
<p><br />
</p>
<pre>½ cup Dijon mustard, 2 tablespoons soy sauce and 1 tablespoon coarsely ground black pepper <br />
</pre>
<p><br />
</p>
<pre>3 tablespoons paprika, 2 teaspoons each coarsely ground black pepper and ground cumin, 1 teaspoon ground nutmeg and ¼ teaspoon salt <br />
</pre>
<p><br />
<br />
HAM <br />
<br />
Baste with mixture of: <br />
<br />
</p>
<pre>¼ cup each orange juice concentrate and rum, 1 tablespoon each honey and brown sugar and ¼ teaspoon each ground cinnamon and ginger <br />
</pre>
<p><br />
</p>
<pre>2/3 cup fruit chutney, 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard and 2 tablespoons lemon juice <br />
</pre>
<p><br />
</p>
<pre>¼ cup each brown sugar and pineapple juice, ½ teaspoon dry mustard and 1 tablespoon cider vinegar <br />
</pre>
<p><br />
</p>
<pre>¼ cup each apricot jam and lemon juice and 1 tablespoon country-style or grainy mustard <br />
</pre>
<p><br />
</p>
<pre>¼ cup honey, 1/3 cup orange juice, 1 tablespoon grated orange zest and ½ teaspoon each dry mustard and ginger <br />
</pre>
<p><br />
</p>
<pre>½ cup apple juice, ¼ cup brown sugar or honey, 2 tablespoons cider vinegar, 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard, ¼ teaspoon cinnamon and 1/8 teaspoon ground cloves <br />
</pre>
<p><br />
LAMB <br />
<br />
Marinades for roasts and chops: <br />
<br />
</p>
<pre>¾ cup orange juice, 1 tablespoon finely grated orange zest, 2 slivered cloves garlic, 1 cup mint leaves, chopped <br />
</pre>
<p><br />
</p>
<pre>¼ cup dry white wine, 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard, ½ teaspoon coarsely ground black pepper, 2 chopped cloves garlic, ¼ cup chopped fresh dill or 1 tablespoon dried dill weed <br />
</pre>
<p><br />
</p>
<pre>1 each minced clove garlic and shallot, ¾ cup each cranberry juice and red wine, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh rosemary or 2 teaspoons dried rosemary, 1 teaspoon whole black peppercorns <br />
</pre>
<p><br />
</p>
<ul><li> Discard any marinade not used to cook the meat. <br />
</li></ul>
<p><br />
<br />
<br />
Rubs and coatings for roasts and racks: <br />
<br />
</p>
<pre>2 tablespoons lemon juice, 1 tablespoon olive oil, 1 minced clove garlic, 1 tablespoon chopped fresh mint or 2 tablespoons mint jelly, ¼ teaspoon each paprika, turmeric, cinnamon, salt and black pepper <br />
</pre>
<p><br />
</p>
<pre>1 tablespoon olive oil, 2/3 cup fresh bread crumbs, 2 tablespoons grainy mustard, 2 teaspoons dried rosemary, ¼ teaspoon each salt and ground black pepper <br />
</pre>
<p><br />
</p>
<pre>½ cup pitted green olives, grated zest of 1 lemon, ¼ cup each minced parsley and mint leaves, 1 tablespoon olive oil, ¼ teaspoon each salt and pepper <br />
</pre>
<p><br />
</p>
<pre>1 ½ teaspoons each ground cumin and paprika, ½ teaspoon salt, ¼ teaspoon ground cloves, 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
</pre>
