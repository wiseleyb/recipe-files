# Ginger Wasabi Glaze

Simple/fast glaze for salmon

## Credits

Ben Wiseley

## Ingredients

- Soy Sauce
- Mirin
- Sriracha
- Wasabi
- Ginger
- Garlic
- Orange juice
- Brown sugar

## Directions

Smash garlic, mince ginger, combine everything in a pan, boil for 10 minutes or
so, strain, brush on salmon while cooking (optional marinate salmon in some)
