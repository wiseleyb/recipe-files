# Negroni cocktail 

<!-- BEGIN content -->

## Credits

by Tom Douglas - Tom's Big Dinners

## Ingredients

- 2 narrow strips lemon zest
- 2 oz gin
- 2 oz Campari
- 2 oz sweet vermouth.

## Directions

Chill glasses in freezer.   
  
 Rub rims with lemon zest, the drop in glass.  
  
 Fill shaker 1/2 full with ice. Pour in liquid, shake and serve.
