# Pan Fried Breaded Sole 

## Credits

by Ben

## Ingredients

- 4 filets per person
- 2 eggs (or more as needed)
- bread crumbs (seasoned with S&P)
- butter
- white wine
- lemon

## Directions

Pan fry lightly breaded sole. Deglaze pan with white wine and lemon (to taste) and reduce with added butter to a sauce.
