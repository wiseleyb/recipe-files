<div class="editsection" style="float:right;margin-left:5px;">[<a href="/recipe/index.php?title=Shitake_Mushroom_Sauce&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Shitake Mushroom Sauce">edit</a>]</div><a name="Ingredients"></a><h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul><li> 2 shallots, chopped fine
</li><li> 1 large garlic clove, minced
</li><li> 2 Tbsp. olive oil
</li><li> 1/2 pound fresh shitake mushrooms, sliced thin
</li><li> 1/3 cup sweet sherry or marsala
</li><li> 1 cup chicken or beef stock
</li><li> 2/3 cup heavy cream
</li><li> 1/4 cup finely minced parsley
</li><li> sea salt to taste
</li><li> fresh ground pepper to taste
</li><li> 1 Tbsp chili oil (optional)
</li></ul>
<p><br />
</p>
<div class="editsection" style="float:right;margin-left:5px;">[<a href="/recipe/index.php?title=Shitake_Mushroom_Sauce&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Shitake Mushroom Sauce">edit</a>]</div><a name="Directions"></a><h2>Directions</h2>
<p>Heat the olive oil in a sauté pan, add the shallots and garlic and soften, add the mushrooms and cook until they soften.  <br />
<br />
Add the sherry or marsala and cook until the mushrooms are cooked.  <br />
<br />
Pour in the stock and cook until the stock has reduced by about 1/2.  <br />
<br />
Pour in the cream and continue to simmer until the sauce is thick.  <br />
<br />
Add the parsley, sea salt, pepper and optional chili oil.<br />
<br />
(serve with steaks)
