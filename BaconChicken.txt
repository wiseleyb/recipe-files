<div class="editsection" style="float:right;margin-left:5px;">[<a href="/recipe/index.php?title=Bacon_Chicken&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Bacon Chicken">edit</a>]</div><a name="Ingredients"></a><h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul><li> 1 1/2 lbs boneless skinless chicken breasts
</li><li> 1 (1 lb) package sliced bacon
</li><li> 3/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
</li><li> 2 tablespoons chili powder
</li><li> 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
</li></ul>
<div class="editsection" style="float:right;margin-left:5px;">[<a href="/recipe/index.php?title=Bacon_Chicken&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Bacon Chicken">edit</a>]</div><a name="Directions"></a><h2>Directions</h2>
<ol><li> Preheat oven to 350°F.
</li><li> Cut chicken breasts into 1-inch cubes.
</li><li> Cut each bacon slice into thirds. Wrap each chicken cube with bacon and secure with a wooden pick inserted on the diagonal starting where bacon seam overlaps.
</li><li> Stir together brown sugar and chili powder. Dredge wrapped chicken in mixture.
</li><li> Coat a rack and broiler pan with non-stick cooking spray. Place chicken on rack in broiler pan.
</li><li> Bake at 350°F for 30 to 35 minutes, or until bacon is crisp.
</li></ol>
