<div class="editsection" style="float:right;margin-left:5px;">[<a href="/recipe/index.php?title=Bacon_Cookies&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Bacon Cookies">edit</a>]</div><a name="Ingredients"></a><h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul><li> 4 slices bacon, cut into small pieces
</li><li> 2 cups all-purpose flour
</li><li> 1/2 cup butter or margarine, chilled
</li><li> 1 egg
</li><li> 1 pinch salt
</li><li> 3 tablespoons heavy cream
</li><li> 1 dash ground black pepper
</li><li> 1 egg yolk, beaten
</li></ul>
<div class="editsection" style="float:right;margin-left:5px;">[<a href="/recipe/index.php?title=Bacon_Cookies&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Bacon Cookies">edit</a>]</div><a name="Directions"></a><h2>Directions</h2>
<ol><li> Fry bacon pieces in a large skillet over medium heat until crispy. Remove with a slotted spoon to paper towels, and cool completely.
</li><li> Into a large mixing bowl, place the flour, butter or margarine, egg, salt, and heavy cream. Using the mixer's hook attachment, mix the contents of the bowl together until combined; then transfer the dough to the counter, and quickly mix with your hands. Knead in cooled bacon pieces and pepper.
</li><li> Roll up the dough into a log, to a diameter of about 1 1/2 inches. Wrap in aluminum foil, and refrigerate for 2 hours, until firm.
</li><li> Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Remove foil, cut dough roll into slices, and place slices on greased baking sheet. Brush each slice with beaten egg yolk.
</li><li> Bake in a preheated oven for 12 to 15 minutes, until brown. Remove to a wire wrack until cooled completely.
</li></ol>
