<div class="editsection" style="float:right;margin-left:5px;">[<a href="/recipe/index.php?title=Roasted_Butternut_Squash_Ravioli_With_Sage%2C_Hazelnut_and_Brown_Butter_Sauce&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Roasted Butternut Squash Ravioli With Sage, Hazelnut and Brown Butter Sauce">edit</a>]</div><a name="Credits"></a><h2>Credits</h2>
<p><a href="http://www.foodtv.com/foodtv/recipe/0,6255,13401,00.html" title="http://www.foodtv.com/foodtv/recipe/0,6255,13401,00.html" class="external text" rel="nofollow">Original Recipe</a>  by Bobby Flay
</p>
<div class="editsection" style="float:right;margin-left:5px;">[<a href="/recipe/index.php?title=Roasted_Butternut_Squash_Ravioli_With_Sage%2C_Hazelnut_and_Brown_Butter_Sauce&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Roasted Butternut Squash Ravioli With Sage, Hazelnut and Brown Butter Sauce">edit</a>]</div><a name="Ingredients"></a><h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul><li> Ravioli:
</li><li> 2 butternut squash, halved and seeded
</li><li> Pinch cinnamon
</li><li> Pinch nutmeg
</li><li> 1 tablespoon finely chopped orange zest
</li><li> 2 teaspoons chipotle puree
</li><li> 1/4 cup finely chopped parsley
</li><li> 1/4 cup finely grated Parmesan cheese
</li><li> Salt and freshly ground pepper
</li><li> 4 sheets pasta dough, 12 to 14 inches long by 4 inches wide
</li><li> 2 eggs mixed with 2 tablespoons water
</li><li> Chopped chives
</li><li> shallots
</li><li> Brown Butter Sauce:
</li><li> 2 sticks unsalted butter
</li><li> 1/4 cup coarsely ground hazelnuts 
</li><li> 1/4 cup heavy cream
</li><li> 8 sage leaves, chiffonade
</li><li> Salt and freshly ground pepper
</li></ul>
<p><br />
</p>
<div class="editsection" style="float:right;margin-left:5px;">[<a href="/recipe/index.php?title=Roasted_Butternut_Squash_Ravioli_With_Sage%2C_Hazelnut_and_Brown_Butter_Sauce&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Roasted Butternut Squash Ravioli With Sage, Hazelnut and Brown Butter Sauce">edit</a>]</div><a name="Directions"></a><h2>Directions</h2>
<p>Ravioli:<br />
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F. Place squash on a baking sheet and roast until soft. Scoop out flesh and run though a food mill. Saute shallots then place the puree into a medium saucepan and cook over low heat until almost dry. Add the remaining ingredients and season with salt and pepper to taste. Lay a sheet of pasta dough on a lightly floured work surface and distribute a heaping teaspoon-sized portion of the filling at least 2 inches apart. Use your fingertip or a brush to moisten the edges of the pasta sheet with the egg wash. Carefully place a second sheet of the pasta dough on top of the first and press with your fingertips to separate the rows of filling. Repeat with the remaining dough. With a ravioli cutter or pastry wheel, cut along straight lines on the vertical and horizontal to form each ravioli square. Press the edges closed with your fingertips to seal well. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Carefully drop in the ravioli and cook for about 5 minutes. Drain, plate and spoon butter sauce over and garnish with finely chopped chives.<br />
<br />
Brown Butter Sauce:<br />
Melt butter in a medium saute pan over medium high heat. Add hazelnuts and cook until the hazelnuts are golden brown. Add the heavy cream and cook for 1 minute. Add the sage and season with salt and pepper, to taste.
