        Grilled Vegetable Lasagna with Puttanesca Sauce and Pesto Oil      </h1>
      <h3 id="siteSub">
        From RecipeWiki      </h3>
      <div id="bodyContent">
        <div id="contentSub">

        <p><br />
</p>
<div class="editsection" style="float:right;margin-left:5px;">[<a href="/recipe/index.php?title=Grilled_Vegetable_Lasagna_with_Puttanesca_Sauce_and_Pesto_Oil&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Grilled Vegetable Lasagna with Puttanesca Sauce and Pesto Oil">edit</a>]</div><a name="Credits"></a><h2>Credits</h2>
<p><a href="http://www.foodtv.com/recipes/re-c1/0,1724,16133,00.html" title="http://www.foodtv.com/recipes/re-c1/0,1724,16133,00.html" class="external text" rel="nofollow">Original Recipe</a>  by Emeril Lagasse
</p>
<div class="editsection" style="float:right;margin-left:5px;">[<a href="/recipe/index.php?title=Grilled_Vegetable_Lasagna_with_Puttanesca_Sauce_and_Pesto_Oil&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Grilled Vegetable Lasagna with Puttanesca Sauce and Pesto Oil">edit</a>]</div><a name="Ingredients"></a><h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul><li> 2 pounds ricotta 
</li><li> 1 teaspoon salt 
</li><li> Pesto Oil, recipe follows 
</li><li> Puttanesca Sauce, recipe follows 
</li><li> 1 1/4 pounds lasagna noodles 
</li><li> Grilled Vegetables, recipe follows 
</li><li> 8 ounces mozzarella, coarsely grated 
</li><li> Puttanesca Sauce: 
</li><li> 1/4 cup olive oil 
</li><li> 1 cup finely chopped onion 
</li><li> 6 cloves minced garlic 
</li><li> 2 (28-ounce) cans Roma plum tomatoes, broken into pieces, with juice 
</li><li> 1 cup tightly packed, pitted, chopped Kalamata olives 
</li><li> 2 tablespoons tomato paste 
</li><li> 2 tablespoons drained capers 
</li><li> 2 tablespoons (about 8) minced anchovy fillets 
</li><li> 1/2 teaspoon dried crushed basil 
</li><li> 1/2 teaspoon dried crushed red pepper flakes 
</li><li> Salt 
</li><li> Pesto Oil: 
</li><li> 2 cloves garlic, minced 
</li><li> 2 cups loosely packed basil leaves 
</li><li> 1 cup extra virgin olive oil 
</li><li> 1 teaspoon salt 
</li><li> Grilled Vegetables:
</li><li> 4 medium zucchini, cut lengthwise into 1/4-inch slices 
</li><li> 4 red or yellow bell peppers, roasted, seeded and peeled 
</li><li> 2 medium eggplants (about 1 1/2 pounds), cut into 1/4-inch rounds 
</li><li> 2 large yellow onions, cut into 1/4-inch rounds 
</li><li> 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
</li></ul>
<p><br />
</p>
<div class="editsection" style="float:right;margin-left:5px;">[<a href="/recipe/index.php?title=Grilled_Vegetable_Lasagna_with_Puttanesca_Sauce_and_Pesto_Oil&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Grilled Vegetable Lasagna with Puttanesca Sauce and Pesto Oil">edit</a>]</div><a name="Directions"></a><h2>Directions</h2>
<p>Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. <br />
<br />
In a medium bowl mix the ricotta cheese with salt and half a cup of Pesto Oil. <br />
<br />
Lightly grease a large rectangular baking dish, then spoon 1/2 cup of Puttanesca Sauce onto the bottom of the dish. Cover with a layer of lasagna noodles. Top the lasagna with a layer of ricotta, then a layer of eggplant, zucchini, onion and red pepper, a layer of grated mozzarella, and a layer of puttanesca sauce. Repeat layering the lasagna, ricotta, vegetables, mozzarella and sauce in this manner until all ingredients have been used, ending with mozzarella on top. <br />
<br />
Bake until the lasagna is bubbling and golden brown, about 1 hour and 15 minutes. Allow to rest 10 minutes before serving, drizzled with some of the remaining pesto oil.<br />
<br />
Puttanesca Sauce:<br />
In a large pot heat the olive oil over medium high heat. Add the onion and sauté until soft and lightly caramelized, about 6 minutes. Add the garlic and cook an additional 2 minutes. Add the tomatoes and the remaining ingredients and simmer until the sauce is thickened and slightly reduced, about 40 minutes. Adjust seasoning to taste, cover and set aside. <br />
<br />
Pesto Oil: <br />
In the bowl of a food processor or blender place the garlic and basil and process on high while adding the olive oil in a steady stream. Continue to process until well blended, season with salt to taste and set aside until ready to assemble lasagna. <br />
<br />
Grilled Vegetables: <br />
Light the grill or pre-heat the broiler. <br />
<br />
In a large shallow bowl, toss the eggplant, zucchini, bell pepper and onion with the oil and salt. If using the broiler, arrange the vegetables in a single layer on 2 lightly greased or non-stick baking sheets. Grill or broil in batches, turning the vegetables once, until they are tender, lightly browned, and have released most of their moisture, about 5 to 6 minutes per side.
</p>
