<ul><li> <a href="/recipe/index.php?title=Empanada_Dough" title="Empanada Dough">Empanada Dough</a>
</li><li> <a href="/recipe/index.php?title=Empanada_Dip:_Mashed_Avocado_with_Lime" title="Empanada Dip: Mashed Avocado with Lime">Empanada Dip: Mashed Avocado_with Lime</a>
</li><li> 3 to 4 waxy red or white potatoes (about 1/2 pound), peeled and sliced 
</li><li> 1 teaspoon salt 
</li><li> 1 pound chorizo sausage, casing removed 
</li><li> 1 Spanish onion, diced 
</li><li> 2 garlic cloves, minced 
</li><li> 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin 
</li><li> 1 teaspoon dried oregano 
</li><li> Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper 
</li><li> 1/2 cup chicken broth 
</li><li> 12 large pimiento-stuffed green olives, chopped 
</li><li> 1/2 cup golden raisins 
</li><li> 1 recipe empanada dough, recipe follows 
</li><li> Cilantro Cream, recipe follows
</li><li> Cilantro Cream 
</li><li> 1 cup sour cream 
</li><li> 1/4 cup finely chopped fresh cilantro leaves 
</li><li> 1/2 lime, juiced 
</li><li> Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
</li></ul>
<div class="editsection" style="float:right;margin-left:5px;">[<a href="/recipe/index.php?title=Empanadas:_Potato_and_Chorizo&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Empanadas: Potato and Chorizo">edit</a>]</div><a name="Directions"></a><h2>Directions</h2>
<p>Put the potatoes in a large pot and cover with cold water; add the salt and bring to a boil, uncovered. Simmer until fork tender, about 15 minutes. <br />
Meanwhile, place a large skillet over medium flame. Add the chorizo, and cook, stirring for 5 minutes, until the sausage renders its fat and gets crispy. Add the onion and garlic; season with the cumin, oregano, salt, and pepper. Cook and stir for 5 minutes, until the mixture is soft and has a beautiful red color. Pour in the broth and simmer for 3 minutes. <br />
<br />
Drain the potatoes and fold them into the pan, using a wooden spoon, break up the potatoes into nickel-size pieces. Add the olives and raisins and toss everything together; season with salt and pepper, to taste. Allow the filling to cool before filling the empanadas. Serve with cilantro cream.<br />
<br />
Cilantro Cream<br />
In a small mixing bowl, combine the sour cream, cilantro, and lime juice together. Mix thoroughly; season with salt and pepper. Serve with empanadas
