        Chinese Chicken Salad with Red Chile Peanut Dressing      </h1>

<div class="editsection" style="float:right;margin-left:5px;">[<a href="/recipe/index.php?title=Chinese_Chicken_Salad_with_Red_Chile_Peanut_Dressing&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Chinese Chicken Salad with Red Chile Peanut Dressing">edit</a>]</div><a name="Credits"></a><h2>Credits</h2>
<p><a href="http://www.foodnetwork.com/food/recipes/recipe/0,1977,FOOD_9936_27273,00.html" title="http://www.foodnetwork.com/food/recipes/recipe/0,1977,FOOD 9936 27273,00.html" class="external text" rel="nofollow">Original Recipe</a>  by Bobby Flay
</p>
<div class="editsection" style="float:right;margin-left:5px;">[<a href="/recipe/index.php?title=Chinese_Chicken_Salad_with_Red_Chile_Peanut_Dressing&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Chinese Chicken Salad with Red Chile Peanut Dressing">edit</a>]</div><a name="Ingredients"></a><h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul><li> 1/4 cup rice wine vinegar 
</li><li> 2 tablespoons smooth peanut butter 
</li><li> 1 tablespoon chopped fresh ginger 
</li><li> 2 teaspoons chipotle pepper puree 
</li><li> 1 tablespoon soy sauce 
</li><li> 1 tablespoon honey 
</li><li> 2 teaspoons toasted sesame oil 
</li><li> 1/2 cup canola oil 
</li><li> Salt and freshly ground pepper 
</li><li> 1/2 head Napa cabbage, shredded 
</li><li> 1/2 head romaine lettuce, shredded 
</li><li> 2 carrots, shredded 
</li><li> 1/4 pound snow peas, julienned 
</li><li> 1/4 cup coarsely chopped fresh cilantro leaves 
</li><li> 1/4 cup thinly sliced green onion 
</li><li> 2 cups shredded rotisserie chicken 
</li><li> 1/2 cup chopped roasted peanuts 
</li><li> 1/4 cup chopped fresh mint leaves 
</li><li> Chili oil, optional 
</li><li> Grilled lime halves, for garnish
</li></ul>
<p><br />
</p>
<div class="editsection" style="float:right;margin-left:5px;">[<a href="/recipe/index.php?title=Chinese_Chicken_Salad_with_Red_Chile_Peanut_Dressing&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Chinese Chicken Salad with Red Chile Peanut Dressing">edit</a>]</div><a name="Directions"></a><h2>Directions</h2>
<p>Whisk together the vinegar, peanut butter, ginger, chipotle pepper puree, soy sauce, honey, sesame oil, and canola oil in a medium bowl. Season with salt and pepper, to taste. Combine cabbage, lettuce, carrots, snow peas, cilantro, and green onion in a large bowl. Add the dressing and toss to combine. <br />
<br />
Transfer to a serving platter and top with the shredded chicken, chopped peanuts, and mint. Drizzle with chili oil, if desired. Garnish with grilled lime halves.
