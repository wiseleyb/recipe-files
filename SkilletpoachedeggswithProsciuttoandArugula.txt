        Skillet-poached eggs with Prosciutto and Arugula      </h1>

        <p><br />
</p>
<div class="editsection" style="float:right;margin-left:5px;">[<a href="/recipe/index.php?title=Skillet-poached_eggs_with_Prosciutto_and_Arugula&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Skillet-poached eggs with Prosciutto and Arugula">edit</a>]</div><a name="Credits"></a><h2>Credits</h2>
<p>by Bon Appetit Jan 2002 pg 112
</p>
<div class="editsection" style="float:right;margin-left:5px;">[<a href="/recipe/index.php?title=Skillet-poached_eggs_with_Prosciutto_and_Arugula&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Skillet-poached eggs with Prosciutto and Arugula">edit</a>]</div><a name="Ingredients"></a><h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul><li> 1 cup finely chopped green onion tops
</li><li> 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
</li><li> 1/4 cup minced fresh Italian parsley
</li><li> 1/2 tsp coarse salt
</li><li> 4 1/2 inch thick slices country style sourdough bread
</li><li> 3 oz arugula leaves
</li><li> 8 thin slices proscuitto
</li><li> 8 large eggs
</li></ul>
<p><br />
</p>
<div class="editsection" style="float:right;margin-left:5px;">[<a href="/recipe/index.php?title=Skillet-poached_eggs_with_Prosciutto_and_Arugula&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Skillet-poached eggs with Prosciutto and Arugula">edit</a>]</div><a name="Directions"></a><h2>Directions</h2>
<p>Combine chopped green onion tops, extra-virgin olive oil, minced Italian parsley and salt in small bowl; whisk to blend.  <br />
<br />
Lightly toast 4 sourdough bread slices.  SPread each with 1 tbs green onion oil.  Arrange 1/4 of arugula on each bread slice.  Top each with 2 slices prosciutto.  Transfer 1 prepared bread slice to each of 4 plates.<br />
<br />
Divide remaining green onion oil equally amoung 4 1.24 cup custoard cups or ramekins.  Using pastry brush, spread green onion oil over inside of each cup to coat (most of mixture will fall back to bottom of cup).  Break open 2 eggs into each prepared cup.  Place cups in large skillet.  Pour enough water into skillet to reach halfway up sides of cups.<br />
<br />
Set skillet over medium-high heat and bring water to simmer.  Reduce heat to medium-low.  Cover skillet and gently cook eggs until whites are just firm to touch and yolks are set to desired consistency, about 6 minutes.<br />
<br />
Using spatula and oven mitt or hand towel as aids, lift cups with eggs from water.  Cut around eggs to loosen.  Turn 2 eggs out onto each prepared bread slice.  Garnish with parsley sprigs.
