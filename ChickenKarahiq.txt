<div class="editsection" style="float:right;margin-left:5px;">[<a href="/recipe/index.php?title=Chicken_Karahiq&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Chicken Karahiq">edit</a>]</div><a name="Ingredients"></a><h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul><li> 6 tablespoons vegetable oil 
</li><li> 1 onion, chopped 
</li><li> 2 cloves garlic, chopped 
</li><li> 1 teaspoon ginger, julienne 
</li><li> 2 to 3 fresh tomatoes, diced 
</li><li> 1 teaspoon salt 
</li><li> 1/2 teaspoon chili powder 
</li><li> 1 green chili, finely chopped 
</li><li> 1 teaspoon ground cumin 
</li><li> 1 teaspoon turmeric 
</li><li> 1 pound chicken, cooked 
</li><li> 1 cup water 
</li><li> 1 1/2 teaspoons garam masala 
</li><li> 1/2 teaspoon dried fenugreek 
</li><li> 1/2 green bell pepper, chopped 
</li><li> 1 tablespoon finely chopped green coriander
</li></ul>
<p><br />
</p>
<div class="editsection" style="float:right;margin-left:5px;">[<a href="/recipe/index.php?title=Chicken_Karahiq&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Chicken Karahiq">edit</a>]</div><a name="Directions"></a><h2>Directions</h2>
<p>Heat oil in a large deep frying pan adding chopped onions and fry for 4 to 5 minutes on a medium heat or once the onions are slightly brown. Add the garlic and ginger and cook for a further 1 or 2 minutes. Add the tomatoes, salt, chili powder, green chili, ground cumin and turmeric. <br />
<br />
Turn up the heat and further cook for another 1 minute then put in the chicken. It is very crucial that spices are mixed within the pan at this point to ensure that the chicken cooks with texture and flavor. Add water after stirring the chicken into the spices. <br />
<br />
Turn down the heat slightly and stir in the garam masala and fenugreek and cook for a further 5 minutes, also adding in the bell pepper stirring now and again. Serve sprinkled with green coriander.
</p><p>Notes:<br />
<br />
Garam Masala can be bought in the international section of some grocery stores or you can make it yourself:  <a href="/recipe/index.php?title=Garam_Masala_Spice" title="Garam Masala Spice">Garam Masala Spice</a>
