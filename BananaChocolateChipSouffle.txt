<div class="editsection" style="float:right;margin-left:5px;">[<a href="/recipe/index.php?title=Banana_Chocolate_Chip_Souffle&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Banana Chocolate Chip Souffle">edit</a>]</div><a name="Ingredients"></a><h2>Ingredients</h2>
<ul><li> Banana Base:
</li><li> 2 ounces butter
</li><li> 1/3 cup brown sugar
</li><li> 1/4 cup sugar
</li><li> 8 ounces, bananas, very ripe, chopped into 1/2-inch pieces
</li><li> 2 tablespoons dark rum
</li><li> 2 tablespoons lemon juice
</li><li> Souffle:
</li><li> 2 tablespoons butter, softened
</li><li> 1/3 cup sugar, for dusting
</li><li> 12 egg whites
</li><li> 4 ounces sugar
</li><li> 2 ounces bittersweet chocolate, chopped into 1/4 inch chips
</li></ul>
<p><br />
</p>
<div class="editsection" style="float:right;margin-left:5px;">[<a href="/recipe/index.php?title=Banana_Chocolate_Chip_Souffle&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Banana Chocolate Chip Souffle">edit</a>]</div><a name="Directions"></a><h2>Directions</h2>
<p>To make the Banana Base: in a medium saucepan, over a high heat, brown the butter. Add the sugars and continue to cook stirring constantly until caramel color, about 5 minutes. Add the chopped bananas and stir until well coated. Continue to cook for 2 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in the dark rum and lemon juice. Transfer mixture to a food processor and pulse until smooth. Transfer to a bowl and allow to cool to room temperature. <br />
<br />
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F. <br />
<br />
Brush the inside of 6 (6-ounce) ramekins with softened butter and dust with sugar. <br />
<br />
To make the souffle: beat the egg whites on medium speed, in a mixer with the whip attachment, to soft peaks. With the motor running, add the sugar in a slow but steady stream. Continue to whip to medium stiff peaks. With a large rubber spatula, fold 1/2 of the meringue into the Banana Base. Add the finely chopped chocolate chips and fold in the remaining 1/2 of the meringue. Fill each prepared ramekin over the top with the souffle mixture. Bake the souffles for 12 minutes. Serve immediately.
